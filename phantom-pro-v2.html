⚡ Signal Rules — When to Trade
  • Signal fires ONLY when ≥3 SMC confluences align at same time
  • Higher timeframe (15min) must confirm trend direction before 5min entry
  • Order Block must be unmitigated (fresh, price revisiting for first time)
  • FVG entry: price must be inside the gap zone, not beyond midpoint
  • Liquidity sweep: wick must be >55% of candle body with close back inside range
  • BOS required before trend-continuation trades
  • CHoCH required before reversal trades
  • Cumulative delta must confirm direction — divergence = skip trade
🛡️ Risk Rules
  • Minimum R:R = 1:2. No signal generated below this threshold
  • SL placed below OB low (BUY) or above OB high (SELL) + small buffer
  • Max 3 concurrent trades — prevents overexposure
  • No new trades after 2:45 PM IST — avoids close-of-day manipulation
  • Capital risk per trade configurable — default 0.5% per trade
📊 Confidence Score (5 pips)
  • Pip 1 — BOS/CHoCH: Market structure break confirmed on 5min
  • Pip 2 — Order Block: Fresh unmitigated OB touched by price
  • Pip 3 — FVG / Sweep: Fair value gap fill or liquidity sweep with rejection
  • Pip 4 — HTF Bias: 15min trend aligns with trade direction
  • Pip 5 — Delta: Cumulative order flow confirms direction
  • Score ≥ 3 → SIGNAL · Score ≤ 2 → NO TRADE
🧠 SMC Concepts
  • Order Block (OB): Last opposing candle before institutional impulse move. Price returns to these zones for entries.
  • Fair Value Gap (FVG): 3-candle imbalance gap. Price returns to fill the inefficiency — acts as magnet.
  • BOS: Break of Structure — higher high/lower low confirming trend continuation by smart money.
  • CHoCH: Change of Character — first structure break against trend, signals institutional reversal.
  • Liquidity Sweep: Price spikes through equal highs/lows to grab retail stops, then reverses sharply.
  • Cumulative Delta: Net buy vs sell volume pressure. Divergence from price = warning signal.
  • Volume POC: Price of Control — highest volume price node. Acts as strong support/resistance.
